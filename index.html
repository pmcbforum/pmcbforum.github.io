    Preface

    REALITY CHECK AS ON DATE :

    • No Entity in sight for merger.
    • No fund infusion or aid by any govt/no help from political parties.
    • Only informal intimation by RBI appointed Administrator about Interest free loan from DICGC for revival plan around 4500 CR.
    • RBI 35A extension letter commits in writing of bank revival exclusively.
    • Administrator informally admits of sending revival plan to RBI.(plan Details unknown).
    • Response from RBI on revival awaited.
    • Practical revival calculations as per known sources makes Bail in option by Equity & Bond conversion unavoidable to restart the Bank.(conversion % not known)
    • Revival does not mean closure of all other options like merger etc. later on.(In fact a pre-step to merger).

    OUR PLANS PROPOSED :

    • Study the merits of the revival plan as & when made available to us by RBI.
    • React on the same with mutual consent of PMC victims subsequently.
    • In the meanwhile Importantly gather the data base in advance of all the bank customers in small whatsapp or telegram groups who are ready to start the bank by accepting the Bail in revival option anticipated from RBI in due course.
    • Reason for this data base collection of like minded bank customers is to speed up the revival process by quickly approaching such groups for updating on revival plans & obtaining their written consents if required later on without waste of time as RBI may give only limited time for raising objections.
    • At this moment we are not planning to give any counter revival plans to RBI. (WAIT AND WATCH FOR RBI PLAN)

    CAUTION : ONE WRONG STEP BY US WILL PUSH BANK INTO LIQUIDATION AND WILL MAKE US BIG LOSERS.

    About

    Though the members were individually involved in the PMCB movement since day one of the crisis, working as a team as a forum or association was conceptualised by the team in November and actions on the team front commenced from December, 2019.

    As per our previous update, the core team of 30 members nominated themselves in the meeting held on 22.12.2019 as volunteers for the forum and the Core group of 12 members took ahead the initiative of the forum by taking immediate actions in the requisite direction.


    CURRENT ACTIVITIES

    Interventions in Criminal cases

    To update you all on the first major task initiated by us as a forum of intervention in the criminal matters, the forum has intervened in the Criminal Bail Applications of the ex- directors Waryam Singh, Jagdish Mookhey, Mukti Bavisi, Trupti Bane, Surjit Singh Arora, Rajneet Tara Singh and Anticipatory Bail Application of Daljit Singh Bal and Gurnam Singh Hothi. All these intervention applications have been allowed by the Mumbai City Sessions Court.

    Besides this, the forum has filed an intervention in the EOW criminal matter on the PMCB Scam-EOW vs. Rakesh Wadhawan & Ors., wherein the charge sheet and the supplementary charge sheets have been filed by EOW.Here too, the intervention Application has been allowed by the CMM, Esplanade Court.

    All the 11 intervention applications filed by us in the name of our 5 warriors (Adv. Hariram Choudhary, Manmohan Singh, Davinder Singh, Virender Singh, Praful Shah). are being argued by our core team member Adv. Prashant Maggu who himself is a PMCB Depositor and Shareholder pro bono. In addition to this one more intervention application was filed in PMLA court to oppose bail applications of Rakesh Wadhawan, Sarang Wadhawan and Warayam Singh.

    Co-ordination with Authorities

    To update you all on the first major task initiated by us as a forum of intervention in the criminal matters, the forum has intervened in the Criminal Bail Applications of the ex- directors Waryam Singh, Jagdish Mookhey, Mukti Bavisi, Trupti Bane, Surjit Singh Arora, Rajneet Tara Singh and Anticipatory Bail Application of Daljit Singh Bal and Gurnam Singh Hothi. All these intervention applications have been allowed by the Mumbai City Sessions Court.
